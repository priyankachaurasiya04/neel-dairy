About Us

Welcome to Neel Dairy — your trusted source for fresh, pure, and high-quality dairy products. Founded in 2025, Neel Dairy is committed to delivering farm-fresh milk and dairy essentials directly to your home.

From milk, curd, butter, paneer, and other dairy products, we focus on hygiene, nutrition, and taste. Our mission is to provide healthy and natural dairy products while ensuring reliability, freshness, and customer satisfaction every day.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver fresh, pure, and high-quality dairy products directly from farms to your home. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of hygiene, nutrition, and taste while supporting local farmers and promoting healthy living for every family.
